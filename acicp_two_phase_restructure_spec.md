# ACICP Two-Phase Restructure Specification

## Requested Change
Restructure the published ACICP curriculum from **6 phases** into **2 phases**, with courses shared as evenly as possible across the two phases.

## Design Principle
The repository currently publishes **12 course sections** and **105 hands-on labs** across a **24-week / 6-month** advanced programme. To satisfy the request for equal sharing where possible, the updated structure should present:

- **Phase 1:** 6 course sections
- **Phase 2:** 6 course sections

This creates an even course split of **6 + 6**.

## Revised Two-Phase Layout
| New Phase | Focus Area | Duration | Courses |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| Phase 1 | Core Security, Network Operations, and Critical Infrastructure | 11 weeks | ACICP 101, ACICP 201, ACICP 202, ACICP 301, ACICP 302, ACICP 303 |
| Phase 2 | Offensive Operations, Cloud Security, and Digital Forensics | 13 weeks | ACICP 401, ACICP 402, ACICP 403, ACICP 601, ACICP 701, ACICP 702 |

## Summary Rules
1. Replace all references to a **6-phase** structure with a **2-phase** structure.
2. Keep the programme duration at **24 weeks (6 months)**.
3. Keep the published totals at **12 course sections** and **105 hands-on labs**.
4. Update tables of contents so only **Phase 1** and **Phase 2** appear.
5. Group the first six course sections under the new Phase 1 heading.
6. Group the remaining six course sections under the new Phase 2 heading.
7. Update FAQ and handbook tables so the course distribution is shown as evenly as possible.
8. Update assessment wording that currently references earlier multi-phase numbering to match the new two-phase model.

## Assessment Mapping Guidance
- The **Mid-Programme Competency Exam** should evaluate **Phase 1** content.
- The **Final Lab Assessment** should evaluate the full **two-phase curriculum**.
- The **Capstone Project & 24-Hour CTF** remains in the final weeks of **Phase 2**.
