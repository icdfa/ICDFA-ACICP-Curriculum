# ACICP Social Media Campaign Kit

**International Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics Academy (ICDFA)**  
**Marketing & Outreach Materials for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and Facebook**

---

## Campaign Overview

This kit provides ready-to-use social media content for promoting the Advanced Critical Infrastructure Cybersecurity Programme (ACICP) across major platforms. All content is professionally written, on-brand, and designed to attract elite cybersecurity professionals.

### Campaign Goals

- Increase awareness of the ACICP among target audience
- Drive applications from qualified cybersecurity professionals
- Establish ICDFA as a leader in critical infrastructure security training
- Build community engagement and thought leadership
- Highlight institutional partnerships and programme benefits

### Target Audience

- Cybersecurity professionals (3–10 years experience)
- Recent graduates with security certifications (Security+, CEH)
- Military and government cybersecurity personnel
- IT professionals transitioning to cybersecurity
- Students in cybersecurity degree programmes

## Best Practices

### Content Guidelines

- **Authenticity:** Use real testimonials and success stories
- **Clarity:** Explain technical concepts in accessible language
- **Consistency:** Maintain brand voice and visual identity
- **Engagement:** Ask questions and encourage comments
- **Timeliness:** Respond to comments and messages within 24 hours

### Posting Best Practices

- **LinkedIn:** Post during business hours (Tuesday–Thursday, 8 AM–5 PM)
- **Twitter/X:** Post during peak hours (8–10 AM, 12–1 PM, 5–6 PM)
- **Facebook:** Post during evening hours (6–9 PM) when engagement is highest

### Engagement Strategy

- Respond to all comments and messages within 24 hours
- Ask follow-up questions to encourage discussion
- Share user-generated content (testimonials, photos)
- Engage with industry news and trending topics
- Collaborate with partners on joint posts
