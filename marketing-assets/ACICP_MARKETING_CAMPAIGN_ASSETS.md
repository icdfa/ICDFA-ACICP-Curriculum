## Distribution Schedule Recommendations

**Phase 1: Awareness (Weeks 1-2)**
- Deploy Campaign Hero on website and email
- Launch LinkedIn and Twitter campaigns
- Share Instagram Stories and Reels

**Phase 2: Engagement (Weeks 3-4)**
- Promote Scholarship Opportunity on all platforms
- Host Info Session with Facebook Event cover
- Increase TikTok and Instagram content frequency

**Phase 3: Conversion (Weeks 5-6)**
- Emphasize application deadline with urgent messaging
- Distribute print flyers to partner institutions
- Launch targeted social media ads
