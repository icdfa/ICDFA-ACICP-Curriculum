## Campaign Messaging Framework

### Primary Headlines

1. **"DEFEND CRITICAL INFRASTRUCTURE"** – Authority, protection
2. **"JOIN THE ELITE"** – Exclusivity, prestige
3. **"MASTER CRITICAL INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY"** – Mastery, expertise
4. **"READY FOR A CHALLENGE?"** – Engagement, motivation
5. **"SCHOLARSHIP OPPORTUNITY"** – Accessibility, support

### Call-to-Action Variations

- **Urgent:** "APPLICATIONS CLOSING SOON"
- **Inviting:** "APPLY NOW"
- **Informational:** "LEARN MORE"
- **Event-Based:** "REGISTER NOW"
- **Social:** "LINK IN BIO"

## Design Specifications

### Typography

- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif, 48-72pt, white (#FCFDFE)
- **Subheadings:** Semi-bold sans-serif, 24-36pt, bright blue (#07ADF6)
- **Body Text:** Regular sans-serif, 14-18pt, off-white (#FCFDFE)
- **Emphasis:** Bold, bright blue (#07ADF6)

### Visual Elements

- Isometric infrastructure illustrations
- Cybersecurity symbols (shields, locks, networks)
- Professional silhouettes (diverse representation)
- Upward arrows (growth, advancement)
- Circuit patterns and network nodes
- Gradient backgrounds (navy to brown)
