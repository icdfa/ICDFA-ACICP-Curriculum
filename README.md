## Programme Overview

The **Advanced Critical Infrastructure Cybersecurity Programme (ACICP)** is a **fully hands-on, lab-driven training programme** designed to develop elite cybersecurity professionals capable of defending **critical national infrastructure (CNI)** including:

*   Power & Energy Systems
*   Oil & Gas Infrastructure
*   Telecommunications Networks
*   Smart Cities & Industrial Systems
*   Cloud-Integrated Infrastructure

This programme focuses on **real-world operational environments**, integrating:

*   Industrial Control Systems (ICS)
*   SCADA Security
*   Offensive & Defensive Cyber Operations
*   Cloud Security Engineering
*   Digital Forensics & Incident Response

## Programme Structure

The programme is divided into **2 Phases** delivered across **24 weeks (6 months)**, with the **12 published course sections** distributed as evenly as possible across the advanced sequence. Phase 1 concentrates on core security, network operations, and critical infrastructure, while Phase 2 concentrates on offensive operations, cloud security, and digital forensics with the final simulation.
